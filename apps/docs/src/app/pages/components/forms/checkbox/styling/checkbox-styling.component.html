Styling

tng-checkbox exposes three klass hooks: rootKlass (label wrapper), inputKlass (checkbox input), labelKlass (label text).

rootKlass

Applies to the root <label>. Default: inline-flex, gap, cursor-pointer.

Example

inputKlass

Applies to the <input type="checkbox">. Use for size and accent.

Example

labelKlass

Applies to the label <span>. Default: text-sm text-fg.
